About us: A family business!

A dream was born when our family decided to return to our Colombian roots after living in Canada. In 2021, we poured our hearts and hands into building La Puesta del Sol, a place nestled in the beautiful coffee region of Filandia, Quindío. Our hotel is conveniently located in the most touristic area of the village, just 200 meters from the lively town center. This hotel is our passion, and we're delighted to share it with you. We're proud to welcome you as part of our family and are ready to assist you in English, French, or Spanish to make your visit unforgettable.
